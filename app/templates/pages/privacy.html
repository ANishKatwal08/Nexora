Privacy Policy

Last updated January 2026

1. Information we collect

When you register, we collect basic details like your name, username, email, and optionally your phone number. We also store the sessions, messages, and reviews you create while using Nexora.

2. How we use your information

We use your information to run the platform, connect you with mentors or learners, send verification codes, and keep your account secure. We do not sell your personal information.

3. Verification codes

Nexora uses two step verification. When you log in, we send a short code to your email to confirm it is really you. These codes expire quickly and are only used to protect your account.

4. Keeping your data safe

Passwords are stored in a securely hashed form, never as plain text. We take reasonable steps to protect your information, though no system can be perfectly secure.

5. Your choices

You can update your profile at any time, and you can delete your account from your settings. Deleting your account removes your personal information from Nexora.

6. Contact

If you have questions about your privacy, please contact us at teamnexora.org@gmail.com.
