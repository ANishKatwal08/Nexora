How can we help?

Find answers to common questions below. If you still need help, reach out to our support team and we will get back to you.

Getting started

Go to Browse mentors from the sidebar. You can filter by category and skill, search by name, and sort by rating. Click any mentor to see their full profile, skills, and reviews.
On a mentor's card or profile, click Request a session. Your request is sent to the mentor, who can confirm it with a time or decline it. You can track all your requests under My requests.
When you register, choose to join as a mentor. Then complete your mentor profile with your profession, headline, rate, experience, and the skills you teach so learners can find you.

Sessions and messaging

Once you have a confirmed session, a Message button appears on that session. You can also message from the Messages area. Both you and the mentor can chat back and forth there.
After a mentor marks a session as completed, you can leave a rating and an optional comment. Your review helps other learners and updates the mentor's average rating.
A pending request can be declined by the mentor if plans change. For any scheduling changes, use messaging to coordinate directly with your mentor.

Account and security

Go to Settings from the sidebar. Under change password, enter your current password and your new one. For your security, we verify your current password before making the change.
In Settings, you can delete your account. You will be asked to confirm by typing DELETE. This permanently removes your account and cannot be undone.
Nexora uses two step verification for your security. After entering your password, we email you a six digit code that is valid for ten minutes to confirm it is really you.
